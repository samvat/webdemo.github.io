About Us

Everyone wants to walk into a home, commercial premises, theatre or a mall that is both warm and welcoming, without compromising on style and durability.
Sai Quantum exports have a collection of all types of floor, wall tiles, wood finish and parking tiles. We also have sanitary wear of exclusive international standards, which suits the requirements of our customers.We also honour tailor made orders as per a specific technical specification & design of a customer incase the order is in bulk.

Our Vision

Sai Quantum Exports' Vision is to be a leader in Customer Ecstasy thus becoming a global leader in the ceramic industry and to upload our quality with new innovative designs to win the trust of our valued customer along with customer relationship.

Our Mission

Sai Quantum Exports is committed to giving world-class products through best quality, technology and customer satisfaction. We strongly believe our customers' growth is our growth. We are therefore becoming the global leaders in exports of the floor, wall, and parking tiles and sanitary wear with support of our customers.

Client Satisfaction

An organization focused on our clients, and we always endeavour to meet clients specific requirements and surpass their expectations with an innovative and upgraded range of Ceramic and Vitrified tiles. We ensure quality products are delivered within the promised time frame.
